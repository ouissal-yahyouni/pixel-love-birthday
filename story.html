OUR STORY 💕

To The Man I Want To Spend My Whole Life With 💍

I still remember the very first time we met—how I fell in love with you and your smile, and how everything happened so unexpectedly. Neither of us planned this; I believe it was God who made it happen because He knew we both needed each other to complete the love story He wrote for us. I am beyond blessed and thankful that someone like you came into my life. ✨


Thank you for coming into my life, for loving me unconditionally, for motivating me every day to feel beautiful, and for always holding my hand so tight that I know you will never let me go. 🤝

I want you to know that:

✦ I want us to grow old together 🌱
✦ I want us to succeed together ❤️
✦ I want us to build dreams and a home filled with love 🏠🤍

I love you with every ounce of my being. Rest assured that you have me, my heart, and my soul. 🏹
